About Us

Rajdhani Tyres™ is a one stop shop for all your wheel related problems. At Rajdhani Tyres™, we make the customers our first priority and take care of their problems as our own. Having served over 15,000 happy customers over a span of over 15 years, we have the experience to deal with every problem.

With a staff that experienced, Rajdhani Tyre is one of the favourites and most trusted shop of the city when it comes to new tyres, and other wheel related services.

What we do

Our services include wheel balancing, changing tyres, nitrogen inflation. We also sell new and original tyres from Bridgestone, Ceat and Goodyear.

Tubeless kits and electronic tyre inflators are also sold here.
