  • Wheel Balancing

    Unbalanced wheel is a very common reason for various road accidents. From decreasing the life of the axle and tyre, to reducing the level of control on your ride, these affect the ride quality a lot and should be fixed without delay.
    At Rajdhani Tyres, wheel balancing is done with the help of zinc weights using a wheel balancing machine which distributes the mass of the wheel uniformly with accuracy.

  • Nitrogen Inflation

    Using nitrogen instead of atmospheric air in the tyres has been proven to be a safer option. From keeping the temperatures lower than air, and expanding comparitively lesser, filling nitrogen in the tyres is the safest option.
    We use the latest and the most efficient nitrogen inflators for this process.
